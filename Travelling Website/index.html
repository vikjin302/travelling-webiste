Popular Travel Destinations

Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is known as the Pink City due to its distinctive architecture. Founded in 1727 by Sawai Jai Singh II, it's one of India's earliest planned cities. With a population of 3.1 million, Jaipur is a popular tourist destination, forming part of the Golden Triangle circuit with Delhi and Agra. It boasts landmarks like Amer Fort and Jantar Mantar, and serves as a gateway to other Rajasthan destinations like Jodhpur and Udaipur. Jaipur was named a World Heritage City in 2019.

Best time to travel: Winters (November to February)

New Delhi

New Delhi, the capital of India and part of the National Capital Territory of Delhi (NCT), hosts the government's three branches and key institutions like Rashtrapati Bhavan and the Supreme Court. Administered by the NDMC, it covers mainly Lutyens' Delhi. Distinct from Delhi, New Delhi forms a small part within the larger megacity. The National Capital Region extends beyond, encompassing adjoining districts and cities like Ghaziabad and Gurgaon. Designed by British architects, New Delhi was inaugurated in 1931 by Viceroy Irwin, following its foundation stone laid by George V in 1911.

Best time to travel: Winters (November to February)
